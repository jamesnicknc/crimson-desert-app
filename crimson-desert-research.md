## Map & Regions

### Continent: Pywel
- Map size: approximately 30-42 square miles (80-110 km2), described as twice the size of Skyrim and larger than Red Dead Redemption 2.
- Five main playable regions plus The Abyss as a special dimension.
- Source: Beebom, GamesRadar [2026-03-15]

### Hernand
- Starting region. Lush green fields, big and small cities, rich mountains and rivers.
- Sub-locations confirmed:
  - Hernand Town Proper
  - The Eldertree
  - Roothold
  - Hernand Highway
  - Nas River
  - Nas River Fishing Dock
  - Antumbra Ritual Grounds
  - Bluemont Manor
  - Hernand Castle (referenced via Kearush the Slayer boss encounter)
- Greymane Camp is located in Hernand (refugee site after Black Bear invasion of Pailune).
- Source: Fextralife Wiki [2026-03-15]

### Pailune
- Northernmost region, Greymane homeland. Harsh mountainous, snowy terrain.
- Features: White Mountains, Frozen Soul Mountain (home of Reed Devil), Dragon Ridge.
- Bosses confirmed here: Staglord, White Horn, Reed Devil.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki, Beebom [2026-03-15]

### Demeniss
- Capital of Pywel and main seat of military and political power.
- King Demeniss's coma sparked a dangerous power vacuum.
- Source: Beebom [2026-03-15]

### Delesyia
- Most scientifically advanced region. Contains mechanical beings, robots, otherworldly constructs.
- Features: Marni's Masterium (location of Golden Star boss), Tesla Ruins.
- Boss: Golden Star (mechanical dragon) protects Marni's Masterium.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki, Game8 [2026-03-15]

### The Crimson Desert
- Barren lawless region of crimson sand. Home to brigands, powerful beasts, ancient secrets.
- Source: Beebom [2026-03-15]

### The Abyss
- A parallel dimension / mysterious realm of pure power, described as "raw and untamed."
- Features: Library of Providence (confirmed location within The Abyss).
- Contains physics-based puzzles; players gain new abilities by solving them.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki, Beebom [2026-03-15]

### Calphade (Newly Revealed Region)
- War-torn battlefield region. Kliff searches for a Greymane comrade here.
- Boss: Cassius Morten (Calphadean traitor) is fought here.
- First shown at PAX West 2025 demo.
- Source: Crimson Desert official Twitter, Sportskeeda [2026-03-15]

### Unicorn Cliffs (Sub-Zone / Point of Interest)
- Home to a guild of Goblins led by Branchmaster Split Horn.
- Located within or near Hernand based on boss context.
- Source: Sportskeeda [2026-03-15]

## Characters

### Kliff Macduff (Playable)
- Primary protagonist. Leader of the Greymane mercenary faction.
- Flexible "play as you like" design. Weapon proficiency spans multiple categories.
- Unique mechanic: Observation Learning. Can learn new skills by watching enemies and NPCs perform moves. Requires spending an Abyss Artifact to permanently add an observed skill to the tree.
- Source: GamesRadar, Beebom [2026-03-15]

### Damiane (Playable)
- Red-headed female character. Glass cannon archetype.
- Weapons: Greatsword, Rapier, Rifle, Pistol. High agility with weapon-switching.
- Unique traversal: uses an umbrella-turned-propeller to glide.
- Source: Beebom, AllThings.how [2026-03-15]

### Oongka (Playable)
- Orc-kin character. Philosopher orc with heavy weaponry focus.
- Weapons: Two-handed Axe, Hand Cannon. Heavy AoE damage playstyle.
- Source: Beebom, DotEsports [2026-03-15]

### Companion NPCs (non-playable, Greymane members)
- **Yann** - Greymane companion NPC (name confirmed, details TBD).
- **Naira** - Greymane companion NPC (name confirmed, details TBD).
- **Marius** - Greymane comrade. Kliff finds him injured (leg wound) hiding in Hernand early in the game. He knows the locations of other scattered Greymane members including Naira and Yann.
- **Russo** - Greymane member. According to Marius, "carried Marius on his back all the way here" and is "still searching for the artifacts." An active Greymane who remained loyal.
- **Ronie** - Operates the Base Camp Food Shop at Greymane Camp.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki [2026-03-15]; Marius and Russo confirmed via YouTube transcript (video 1ItNYP9U1M0) [2026-03-15]

### Other Named NPCs
- **Aliston** - Oversees the Library of Providence in the Abyss. Describes the Abyss as "the center of all worlds" where records of all Providence exist. Tasks Kliff with correcting Rifts that bring chaos. Appears early in the Abyss storyline.
- **White Crow** - "Guardian of the Abyss." Appears alongside Aliston and teaches Kliff flying/gliding mechanics in the Abyss. Described as a bridge between Providence and the world.
- **Bon Midler** - Official "responsible for keeping peace in Hernand." Sends Kliff on early Hernand quests including investigating the Sky Boulder incident and the Red Smoke bandits.
- **Shakatu** - Merchant NPC and crossover character from Black Desert. Becomes the new Branch Master of the Gold Leaves goblin guild after Kliff defeats Split Horn. Described as "a merchant through and through."
- Source: YouTube transcript (video 1ItNYP9U1M0) [2026-03-15]

## Greymane Camp

### Location
- Located in Hernand. Serves as refugee camp after the Black Bears drove the Greymanes from Pailune.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki [2026-03-15]

### Facilities Confirmed
- **Trading Center** - Exchange goods for useful exploration items.
- **Cooking / Bonfire Kitchen** - Prepare food and rations.
- **Base Camp Food Shop** - Buy/sell food, rations, and cooking provisions. Operated by NPC Ronie.
- **Personal Resting House** - Customizable player dwelling for recovery.
- **Barber Shop** - Modify hairstyle, beard, eyebrows, face tattoos, body tattoos.
- **Dyehouse Shop** - Change outfit colors and customize mount colors (horse, mech).
- **Crop Farm Plots** - Grow ingredients for cooking and equipment upgrades.
- **Livestock Ranch** - Raise cows, pigs, goats, chickens for materials.
- **Dispatch Board** - Send Greymane members on missions to reduce enemy numbers in occupied zones; earn currencies and resources on return.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki [2026-03-15]

### Camp Customization
- Fully customizable player base. Players can manually place objects and items to design the headquarters. Can expand buildings, add facilities, and furnish for functional and cosmetic improvements.
- Source: Gurugamer [2026-03-15]

### NPCs
- Surviving Greymane members gradually move into the camp as the story progresses.
- Ronie operates the Base Camp Food Shop.
- Source: Fextralife Wiki [2026-03-15]

### Camp Upgrade Materials (General -- Run 10)
- Resources needed for camp upgrades are acquired through general gameplay progression.
- Greymane Dispatch missions can bring back timber, ore, rare materials, and Abyss Remnants.
- Source: game-data.ts existing entries; gurugamer.com [2026-03-16]

## Skills & Skill Trees

### System Overview
- No traditional XP system. Skills unlocked through: exploration, defeating enemies, solving puzzles, observing NPCs/enemies, and boss encounters.
- Currency: Abyss Artifacts (spent to unlock or upgrade skills).
- Each character has an independent skill tree with multiple branches.
- Abyss Gears slot into equipment for additional stat bonuses (attack speed, damage, crit chance).
- Source: Games.gg, GamesRadar [2026-03-15]

### Kliff Skill Details (Confirmed Branches)
- Sword Mastery, Unarmed Combat, Ranged & Bow, Abyss Magic, Vitality.
- Observation Learning: skills like Belly Slam (from sumo wrestler) and Force Palm (from holographic projection) acquired by watching NPCs or enemies.
- Source: game-data.ts (existing app data) [2026-03-15]

### Kliff Skill Tree Color Branches (New Detail)
- Three color-coded skill branches confirmed:
  - **Blue branch**: Combat moves -- spin attack (R1 + face button), charged stab, unarmed combo extensions, archer moves, drop kick (air-only move), and more.
  - **Green branch**: Spirit/focus tree -- includes a "focus move" that slows time and pushes enemies away; spirit cost on certain skills.
  - **Red branch**: Grapple hook and elemental enhancements. Includes the "VO" element (smoke bomb abilities).
- "VO" element: A new undocumented elemental type. Allows deployment of smoke bombs with various skill variations. NOT previously documented anywhere.
- Pump Kick: Can be observed from an enemy knight boss during an early fight and immediately stolen as a learnable move.
- Source: YouTube transcript (video k-yOpdOk_a0) [2026-03-16]
- Transcript excerpt: "...the one element we did not know about yet is called VO, which lets you deploy basically smoke bombs with various skills..."

### Damiane Skill Details (Confirmed Branches)
- Blade Arts, Gunplay, Evasion, Abyss Spells.
- Source: game-data.ts [2026-03-15]
- Additional weapon confirmed: Musket (delayed, high-power shots; distinct from Rifle) -- alcasthq.com, Damiane guide [2026-03-16]
- Izuna Drop: Confirmed signature execution move for Damiane.
- Claymore moveset: Sweeping wide arcs into spinning slashes, slightly faster but less per-hit damage than a standard greatsword.
- "Steel Rose" build: Rapier + Pistol focus. Rapier described as "fast, precise thrusts that target weak points for critical damage."
- Source: alcasthq.com, search result (2026-03-16)

### Oongka Skill Details (Confirmed Branches)
- Axe Fury, Wrist Cannon, Fortitude, Vitality.
- Source: game-data.ts [2026-03-15]
- Hammer is confirmed as a third weapon type for Oongka (in addition to Axe and Arm Cannon). The app's current WEAPONS data only lists Axe and Hand Cannon for Oongka.
- "Mountain Breaker" build: Hammer + Arm Cannon focus. Hammer emphasizes crowd knockback.
- "Siege Titan" build: Great Axe + Arm Cannon. Great Axe for spinning AoE.
- Body slam and leap slam: Confirmed signature moves for Oongka.
- Source: alcasthq.com search results (2026-03-16)

### Weapon Tier List (Run 10 -- Pre-Launch Editorial Ranking)
- **S Tier**: Sword (most balanced, fast combos, reliable defense), Dual Blades (fastest attack chains, high combo damage, requires stamina management)
- **A Tier**: Spear (long reach, safer vs large enemies, thrust attacks), Greatsword (massive per-hit damage, high stagger, slow speed)
- **B Tier**: Bow (safe ranged, lower burst damage), Heavy Blunt Weapons (strong stagger vs armored enemies, slow)
- Beginner recommendation: Sword
- Source: crimsondeserthub.com tier list [2026-03-16]

### Skill Unlock Notes
- Most major abilities (including flying) are tied to defeating specific bosses.
- Some skills are gated behind specific NPC interactions in the world.
- "Crow's Wing" gliding mechanic confirmed as an early unlockable traversal skill. Recommended to unlock early. Combines with grappling hook for mountain traversal.
- Simplified Combos toggle available in settings for players who find multi-button inputs difficult.
- Source: Beebom, GamesRadar [2026-03-15]; Crow's Wing from crimsondesert.club beginner guide [2026-03-16]

## Game Mechanics (New Discoveries)

### Bounty / Crime System
- Notice boards and wanted posters scattered through regions launch bounty quests.
- Mechanics: Find the target, tackle and tie them up, escort them to prison. Targets may flee on sight.
- Random event: NPCs can pickpocket Kliff; Kliff can chase and tackle them.
- Reward: Faction currency redeemable at a dedicated faction shop.
- Crime system: Attacking NPCs or guards triggers town-wide hostility. Guards are high-level enemies early in game. Getting caught sends Kliff to prison (significant silver fine based on crime severity).
- Trust score: Increases (+5 per greeting, helping citizens, etc.) and decreases with criminal activity.
- Mask mechanic: A mask (reward from first bounty) is required to steal from NPCs or access certain disguised interactions.
- Faction map tab: Shows areas controlled by each faction; completing missions raises faction loyalty and unlocks perks.
- Source: YouTube transcript (video k-yOpdOk_a0) [2026-03-16]

### Faction Shops
- Dedicated shops run by factions that accept faction-specific currency (earned from bounties/faction missions).
- Contents confirmed (early faction shop): Cool armor sets, a banner, spear, horse armor.
- To access one shop, Kliff had to change outfits to get past faction guards (equipment-gated access).
- Source: YouTube transcript (video k-yOpdOk_a0) [2026-03-16]

### Mount Leveling
- Mounts can level up through use. Leveling unlocks new combat moves.
- Confirmed new mount move from leveling: A kick attack that pushes enemies away while mounted.
- Source: YouTube transcript (video k-yOpdOk_a0) [2026-03-16]
- Transcript excerpt: "...your mount can actually level up in this game, unlocking new moves like a kick to push enemies away..."

### Abyss Physics Powers (Combat/Traversal)
- Force-style object manipulation: Push and pull heavy objects using Abyss power, lift items with weightlessness.
- Force Jump: Propels Kliff off the ground for combat jumps and traversal.
- These powers are unlocked in the Abyss islands (sky realm) during early story missions.
- Cost: Spirit resource (slowly regenerates; cannot spam).
- Source: YouTube transcript (video LqXzIcc4-0o, IGN) [2026-03-16]

### Side Activities / Quests (New Confirmed)
- Chimney Sweep quest: NPC fell from roof, needs Kliff to climb up and use a broom to clear chimney.
- Rescue Sheep quest: Abducted sheep must be retrieved to cheer up an upset child.
- Rock Paper Scissors minigame: Available in back alleys with local ruffians.
- Source: YouTube transcript (video LqXzIcc4-0o, IGN) [2026-03-16]

### Technical Details (Additional)
- AMD FSR Redstone support: Crimson Desert is the second game to support AMD FSR Redstone (includes Upscaling, ML Frame Generation, Ray Regeneration).
- Water simulation: FFT Ocean simulation and Shallow Water simulation for realistic waves, currents, ripples, and puddle persistence after rain.
- Rain persistence: Puddles remain for hours of in-game time after rain stops; rain dynamically starts/stops.
- Displacement mapping: Used extensively on virtually every surface for extreme texture depth.
- 3 PS5 Pro modes: Optimal (1080p/60fps), Balanced (1440p/40fps), Quality (4K/30fps). PSSR2 confirmed for launch day.
- Bug Hill area: Named large-scale battle area featuring mechanical insects vs. gun-wielding soldiers and orc forces. Frame-rate intensive area. Possibly in Delesyia or a border region.
- Orc City on mountain: A heavily fortified orc settlement atop a mountain, visually distinct with pipes and ornate structures.
- Circus: A random lively circus event found in the open world with active performances inside a tent.
- Source: YouTube transcript (video 3uUIBUoJhk8, Digital Foundry) [2026-03-16]
